FREQUENTLY QUESTIONS

Below are frequently asked questions, you may find the answer for yourself.

Here you will find a list of frequently asked questions relating to The Oak store. Still can't find your answer? Send us a message.

Can you wrap my order?

We offer wrapping on all ordered items

Do you deliver on weekends?

Yes, we do. Our delivery timelines are between 9:00 am and 4:00 pm on Saturday. While on Sundays from 11:00 am to 4:00pm

How to place my order?

You will need to send your contact details and address through our whatsapp button, choose a delivery option and then make payment.

What Payment Methods are Available?

We have an mpesa till no for those who wish to pay online. We also accept cash payments on delivery

Are returns free?

Returns processing may take up to 14 days. Refunds are made to the purchaser using the original method of payment.
